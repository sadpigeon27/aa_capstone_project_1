About Teddy:

Meet Teddy, a 2-year old black and white cat we adopted recently. He likes rolling on the floor picking up all the dust, laying across doorways and screaming when he's bored. He's an incredibly playfull yet surprisingly lazy cat with a friendly nature. The decision to adopt him was a spontaneous one, yet we never regretted it, as he brought some long-lacking fun and activity into our home, and we are happy to give him back with all our love.
